# Tickets

Tickets are the core of Zip Station. Every customer conversation becomes a ticket that your team can track, assign, and resolve.

## Creating a Ticket

Click **New Ticket** from the ticket list. Fill in the customer email, subject, and your message. The ticket is created and the customer receives your reply.

## Ticket List

The ticket list shows all tickets for the current project. Use the toolbar to filter by:

- **Status** — Open, Pending, Resolved, Closed
- **Priority** — Low, Medium, High, Urgent
- **Assignment** — Assigned to you, unassigned, or a specific team member
- **Search** — Find tickets by subject, content, or ticket ID

## Working on a Ticket

Open a ticket to see the full conversation thread. From here you can:

- **Reply** — Send a response to the customer
- **Internal Note** — Add a private note only your team can see
- **Change Status** — Move the ticket through the workflow: Open, Pending, Resolved, or Closed
- **Assign** — Assign the ticket to yourself or a team member
- **Set Priority** — Change the priority level
- **Add Tags** — Label tickets for organization
- **Link or Merge** — Connect related tickets or merge duplicates

## Indicators

- **Customer Replied** — A badge appears when the customer has responded and the ticket needs attention
- **Stale Ticket** — Tickets without recent activity are flagged so nothing falls through the cracks

## Bulk Actions

Select multiple tickets from the list to change status, assign, or update priority in bulk.
