# Settings

The Settings page lets you configure your Zip Station workspace.

## Appearance

- **Theme** — Choose between Light, Dark, or System (follows your OS preference)
- **Language** — Switch the dashboard language

## Company Settings

- **Dashboard URL** — The URL where your Zip Station instance is hosted. Used in email links and notifications.
- **Company SMTP** — Configure the SMTP server used for system emails like team invitations. This is separate from the per-project SMTP used for ticket replies.

## Team Members

View and manage your team:

- **Member List** — See all team members, their roles, and status
- **Invite Members** — Send an invitation email to add someone to your team. They'll receive a link to create their account.
- **Resend Invites** — If an invitation expired or was lost, resend it with one click
- **Remove Members** — Remove a team member from your workspace

## Roles

Manage roles from the Settings page. See [Roles and Permissions](/docs/user-guide/roles-and-permissions) for details on creating and assigning roles.
