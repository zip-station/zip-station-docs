# Dashboard

The Dashboard gives you a quick snapshot of your support activity. It's the first page you see after logging in.

## Ticket Counts

At the top, you'll see summary cards showing:

- **Open** — Tickets actively being worked on
- **Pending** — Tickets waiting for a customer reply
- **Resolved** — Tickets marked as resolved
- **Closed** — Tickets that are fully closed

These counts update in real time as your team works through the queue.

## Response Time Stats

Below the counts, you'll find average response time metrics. This helps you track how quickly your team is replying to customers.

## Charts

The dashboard includes visual charts for:

- **Tickets by Status** — See the distribution of open, pending, resolved, and closed tickets
- **Tickets by Priority** — Understand how many tickets fall into each priority level (Low, Medium, High, Urgent)

Use these charts to spot trends and allocate your team's time effectively.
