# Customers

The Customers page gives you a directory of everyone who has contacted your support team.

## Customer List

Browse all customers with search and filtering. Each entry shows the customer's email, name (if known), and how many tickets they have.

## Customer Detail Page

Click on a customer to see their full profile:

- **Ticket History** — Every ticket associated with this customer, across all projects
- **Properties** — Email address, name, and any custom properties
- **Tags** — Label customers for organization (e.g., "VIP", "Enterprise")
- **Notes** — Add internal notes about the customer that your whole team can see

This gives your team context before responding to a ticket — you can see past interactions and any relevant notes at a glance.
