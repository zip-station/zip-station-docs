# Getting Started

Welcome to Zip Station! This page walks you through your first login and the basics of navigating the dashboard.

## First Login

After your admin sets up Zip Station, you'll receive an invitation email. Click the link, create your account, and sign in. You'll land on the **Dashboard**.

## Navigation

The left sidebar is your main navigation. Here's what you'll find:

- **Dashboard** — Overview of ticket stats and charts
- **Tickets** — Your ticket list with filters and search
- **Intake** — Incoming emails waiting for review
- **Customers** — Contact list and customer details
- **Projects** — Manage separate support channels
- **Settings** — Team, theme, and company configuration
- **Audit Log** — Activity history

## Project Selector

At the top of the sidebar, you'll see a **project selector**. If your team uses multiple projects (e.g., separate inboxes for Sales and Support), switch between them here. Your ticket list and intake queue update to show only that project's items.

## Theme and Language

Go to **Settings** to switch between light, dark, or system theme. You can also change the dashboard language from the same page.

## Next Steps

Head to the [Dashboard](/docs/user-guide/dashboard) page to learn about the stats and charts available, or jump to [Tickets](/docs/user-guide/tickets) to start managing support requests.
